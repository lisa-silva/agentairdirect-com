Common Questions

What Contractors Ask Us

Do you need access to my website to fix it?
Yes — we’ll need basic access to add your structured data and visibility signals. We guide you step‑by‑step, and we never modify anything outside the AI Search Visibility implementation. WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, GoHighLevel, and most builders are simple.
How long until I start showing up in AI results?
Most clients begin appearing in AI systems within the first few weeks. Perplexity and Google AI often update fastest, while ChatGPT and Siri take longer as they re‑crawl and re‑index your site.
I already have SEO — isn't that the same thing?
No. SEO improves Google search rankings. AI Search Visibility improves how AI engines understand, classify, and recommend your business. Most SEO work does not include entity repair, schema integrity, Knowledge Graph alignment, or AEO/GEO optimization — the signals AI systems rely on.
What if I don't have a website at all?
We can still help. Your Google Business Profile and core entity signals can be optimized even without a website. We’ll put together a plan based on your situation.
What kinds of contractors do you work with?
Plumbers, roofers, HVAC, electricians, general contractors, landscapers, painters, garage door techs, pest control — any local service business that needs more calls.
What exactly are you fixing on my site?
We repair your entity signals, structured data, Knowledge Graph alignment, service‑area clarity, trust indicators, and AEO/GEO visibility signals — the elements AI engines use to understand and recommend your business.
Will this help me show up for “near me” searches?
Yes. GEO optimization strengthens your service‑area signals so AI systems can match you to local intent queries like “plumber near me” or “best roofer in my area.”
Do you rewrite my content?
Not unless your plan includes it. Most visibility improvements come from entity, schema, and Knowledge Graph fixes — not rewriting your website.
Is this a one‑time fix or ongoing?
Starter Fix and Pro are one‑time implementations. If you want ongoing updates as AI engines evolve, the Stay Visible plan handles monthly maintenance.
Does this replace SEO?
No — it complements it. SEO helps with Google search. AI Search Visibility helps with ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, Copilot, and Google AI Overviews.
Can you work with my agency?
Yes. Many agencies use AgentAir as their AI Search Visibility backend. We can deliver the implementation while your agency handles everything else.
