AgentAirDirect was built by Lisa Silva after seeing a growing gap between how businesses describe themselves online and how AI systems interpret that information during discovery.

As AI search began changing how people ask for recommendations, compare options, and find local services, Lisa focused on one practical problem: many businesses have information online, but it is incomplete, disconnected, or difficult for machine systems to interpret clearly.

That conviction led her to advocate for AI Search Visibility as a distinct category. When existing G2 classifications did not accurately describe what AgentAir was built to do, Lisa documented the gap, explained the difference, and formally made the case for a separate AI Search Visibility category. G2 later approved AgentAir Intelligence Suite for listing in that category.

AgentAirDirect turns that gap into a visibility system: identifying what is unclear, connecting the information that matters, and creating a stronger machine-readable foundation around the business.

The goal is simple: give businesses and agencies a clearer path from AI visibility diagnosis to practical action.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AgentAir?

AgentAirDirect is an AI Search Visibility Engine. Its Signal Audit reviews public-facing business information for verified gaps and creates practical corrections where appropriate.

What is an AI Search Visibility Engine?

An AI Search Visibility Engine is a platform that identifies and improves the technical, content, entity, and trust signals AI systems use when forming answers.

What does the AgentAir Signal Audit check?

The Signal Audit evaluates structured data, content clarity, FAQ coverage, business identity, technical readiness, and trust information.

Why does FAQ schema matter for AI search?

FAQ schema gives search systems clearly structured questions and answers that are easier to interpret when relevant.

What is Golden River Connect?

Golden River Connect is the methodology that connects identity, services, location, trust, customer questions, visible content, structured data, interpretation, and the customer’s next step.

What does the Persona Engine do?

The Persona Engine auto-detects your business type — dental, med-spa, real estate, legal, restaurant, contractor, or agency — and adjusts every signal, score, and output accordingly.

What schema types can AgentAir help generate?

AgentAir can help generate schema such as SoftwareApplication, Product, Organization, LocalBusiness, FAQPage, and industry-specific Schema.org types.

Who founded AgentAir?

AgentAirDirect was founded by Lisa Silva to help businesses build clearer, connected foundations from verified public information.
