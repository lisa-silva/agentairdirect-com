Module 11

Order Online
Button Generator

Turn AI discovery into a real order.

Detects ordering and checkout links on a business website, validates the destination and flow, and generates an installable Order Online button that customers and AI agents can trust.

What this module does

  • Scans for ordering and checkout URLs
  • Validates that the destination is first-party and functional
  • Flags broken, redirected, or unsafe links
  • Generates an installable Order Online button with AI agent metadata
  • Enforces safe fallback if ordering cannot be verified

Outcome

"Turns 'AI found your business' into
'customer placed an order.'"
